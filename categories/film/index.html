Cloud Atlas

31/Jan 2016 2 min. read

film

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ A daring work of art showcasing what the magic of cinema has to offer! “Cloud Atlas” manages to be a science-fiction, historical drama, action, mystery, thriller, romance, comedy, and much more. There are six different story lines, spanning from the 1800’s to the 2300’s or so, all told together beyond the bounds of narrative continuity. The same actors are used in multiple story lines, disguised in makeup and costumed so cleverly that sometimes they are unrecognizable.

Deux Jours Une Nuit

12/Jan 2016 2 min. read

film

★ ★ ★ ★ “Deux Jours, Une Nuit” (Two Days, One Night) reminds us of the people that are negatively affected by the growing wealth gap and the deteriorating middle class. The Dardenne brothers offer yet another important view into the world of the struggling, everyday Belgian worker. The main character, Sandra (Marion Cotillard), is a young Belgian mother who is recovering from clinical depression. However, she returns from her sick leave to find that her boss has given her 16 coworkers a choice: receive 1000 euro bonuses or let Sandra return and keep her job.

film
