First Post - Goals

12 Dec 2015 on Admin

With fall quarter officially over, I finally found the time to get this site up and make it look decent (I hope). For my first post, I just wanted to type out what I hope this website/blog can be:

  • A Personal Website
    • Since I am a computer science student, I should probably have one of these right? Something about demonstrating my capabilities and what not. Thus, I'll have another link to my resume and my github.
  • A Creative Writing Space
    • In the style of a blog. A blog about what you might ask? I'm not quite sure yet. I could post something about my life, a short story, advice on various topics, my opinion of current events, anything really. My mind can't seem to settle on one niche topic, so for now this blog will remain very open.
  • A Film Journal
    • I really like film. I like film so much that as I type this, I'm still considering whether I should switch to a Radio, TV, Film (RTVF) major. I'll be posting my reviews of films I watch in the future, and maybe write some film related essays/guides.

Hopefully I'll be posting here often; my last wordpress blog has been dormant for a few years now. To prevent this site from suffering the same fate, I'm making it my goal is to post at least once a week, starting with the new year. Looking forward to a new blogging adventure! Stay tuned for more later.

Kevin Li
